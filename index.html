🏆 Pig Game Rules 🎲

  • Objective: Be the first to reach 50 points or more to win.
  • Rolling the Dice:
    • Rolling a number adds it to your current score.
    • Press Hold to save your current score to your total and pass the turn.
  • Rolling a 1: Your current score resets to 0, and the turn passes to the other player.
  • Active Player: Has a lighter background and bold font.
  • Winning: The first player to reach 50 or more points wins! 🎉
  • New Game: Click New Game to restart.
